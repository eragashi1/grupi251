dua Lipa

Dua Lipa is an English and Albanian[a] singer and songwriter.
Her voice and disco-influenced production have received critical acclaim and media coverage.
Her accolades include seven Brit Awards and three Grammy Awards.
Time magazine included her in its list of the top 100 most influential people in the world for 2024.
