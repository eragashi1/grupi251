Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM (Portuguese pronunciation: [kɾiʃˈtjɐnu ʁɔˈnaldu]; born 5 February 1985)
is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays
as a forward for and captains both Saudi Pro League club Al Nassr and the Portugal national team.
